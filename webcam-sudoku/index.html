Sudoku detection from web camera

Detects sudoku puzzles from web camera and recognizes the digits in the puzzle.
  • Click "Start camera" to start your web camera.
  • Show a sudoku puzzle to web camera.
  • When the puzzle digits are recognized, the detected puzzle is shown with its solution.
  • Click "Show solution" button if you want to see more detailed steps and techniques how to solve the puzzle.
  • Click "Stop camera" to stop recording at any time.
The detection runs on your web browser only. No data is sent or being collected.
